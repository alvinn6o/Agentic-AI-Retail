# Restock Policy SOP

## Trigger Conditions
- Restock when inventory falls below 2 weeks of forecasted demand
- Priority restock when stockout risk > 70% within next 7 days

## Approval Thresholds
- Orders under £500: Warehouse manager can approve
- Orders £500–£5,000: Operations manager approval required
- Orders over £5,000: Director sign-off required

## Lead Times
- Standard suppliers: 5–10 business days
- Express suppliers: 2–3 business days (15% premium applies)
- International suppliers: 15–30 business days

## Preferred Action
Always prefer standard lead-time orders unless stockout is imminent.
Maintain a minimum buffer stock of 10% above forecasted weekly demand.
